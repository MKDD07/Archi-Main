Overview

Vibhor Sogani is an internationally acclaimed Indian artist and designer, known for his large-scale public art installations that explore the interplay of light, space, and culture. Born in Jaipur, he has been living and working in New Delhi for over 25 years, crafting narratives through light and steel sculptures.

Educated at the National Institute of Design, India, Sogani’s works aim to transform urban environments, inviting viewers to connect with their surroundings in meaningful ways.

Vision & Artistic Philosophy

Sogani’s art engages with space, environment, and community. His creations often use stainless steel and reflective surfaces to interact with light and surroundings, transforming public spaces into dynamic, contemplative experiences.

His philosophy emphasizes accessibility, site-specific expression, and a connection between tradition and contemporary aesthetics, making urban environments more vibrant and meaningful.

Global Recognition & Exhibitions

Vibhor’s works have been exhibited internationally in countries such as Netherlands, Australia, Zimbabwe, Tanzania, South Africa, Germany, and Denmark. His public art installations resonate with both local communities and global audiences.

He has been invited as a keynote speaker and juror for prestigious design forums and conferences, sharing his insights on art, design, and public engagement.

Awards & Leadership

Vibhor Sogani has received several accolades, including the Lifetime Design Excellence Award (2025) and Indian Art Icon of the Year 2014. He serves as a member of the governing body of the Indian Design Council.

His works continue to inspire and shape public art practice globally, blending creativity, culture, and community engagement.
