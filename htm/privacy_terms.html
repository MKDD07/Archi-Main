Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We may collect both personal data and non-personal data about you when you interact with our website. The information collected includes:

a) Personal Data:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.

b) Non-Personal Data:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as pages viewed, time spent on the site, browser type, IP address, and referring site.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files placed on your device, allowing us to understand how you interact with our website and improve its functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes, in line with the practices of leading designers and design firms:

  • To Provide Services: To process and respond to inquiries, orders, or collaborations related to our installations and artwork.
  • To Improve Our Website: We analyze data to enhance the performance and user experience of our website, ensuring that it reflects the high standards of design and accessibility.
  • To Communicate with You: To send newsletters, updates about exhibitions, product launches, and other marketing communications, if you have opted in to receive them.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To fulfill any legal obligations or defend against claims, where necessary.

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect the privacy of your information and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., website hosting, email marketing services, payment processors) to help us operate the website and provide services. These providers have access to your information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect it.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your personal information in response to a valid legal request or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Vibhor Sogani, our clients, or others.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures that are aligned with global standards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access: The right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: The right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain conditions.
  • Objection: The right to object to the processing of your personal data for marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including online galleries, press features, or design collaborations. These websites are governed by their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

7. International Data Transfers

As a globally recognized design practice, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. We take steps to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

8. Retention of Data

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time, reflecting changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

10. Contact Us
